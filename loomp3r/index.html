Pause (sec)
loomp3r

This will let you loop over sections of an audio file and optionally pause before. To start choose a file. Create sections by dragging over the wave. Clicking a section will loop it. Clicking outside a section will toggle play/pause. Section you add are saved in localStorage, and you can clear those for a particular (Clear) or for a files (Clear All). You can also restore the sections after creating them.
